How it works

Navigate through three different tabs:

  1. A Team Selection tab where you can enter your team and will be recommended transfers
  2. A Transfer Analyser tab where you can analyse proposed transfers
  3. A Top Performers tab where you can visualise the top performers for future Gameweeks

How it actually works
The proposed transfers are based on the outputs of a machine learning model
using a recurrent neural network. This model is created for every player using previous game
week stats (up to 230) to predict the next game week points. We are currently predicting for GW
{{gameweek}}. In addition to this, the model incorporates simple Bayesian statistics to determine
likelihood of these predictions and adjusts them accordingly.

*predictions are affected by the current cost of your team thus recommendations from the team selection page may not be possible
